Nash Equilibrium Analysis

In this game, there are two pure Nash Equilibria:

  • (Go, Stop): Player 1 goes, Player 2 stops
  • (Stop, Go): Player 1 stops, Player 2 goes

In these situations, neither player can improve their payoff by unilaterally changing their strategy.
